Unemployed and Loving It Log

When life gives you lemons, make lemonade!

Introduction: How we got here

Hi everybody, my name is Joy and I just got laid off from my job two days ago. It wasn't much of a surprise; my most recent occupation had been doing customer service for an international tour company that, of course, had to scale down in order to cope with a dramatic loss of revenue caused by the pandemic.

As one might imagine, working customer service in such a situation was less than optimal, and as the months went by, I realized that I needed to get out. I wasn't going to quit (I felt too scared to do that in this uncertain job climate), but I had begun looking at my options and made the decision to continue schooling to get a better opportunity for myself. I had finally decided to pursue a more technical career and, no kidding, enrolled in some online night classes just the night before I got let go. While those classes were more geared toward analytics, I knew I wanted to learn programming and now, with all of this job that I no longer have, I'm going to squeeze out as much lemonade as I can.

This log is going to be where I track my progress with finding a job and pursuing a new skillset, God willing, leading to a new career.
